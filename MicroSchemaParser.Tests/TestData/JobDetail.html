API Developer - Glasgow

£24,000 - £32,000 + healthcare, flexitime, free beer, early Friday finish and more! - Product Development
Ref: 45 Date Posted: Tuesday 09 Jan 2018
More

Are you an API Developer who's looking for an opportunity to bring lots of technology together; identify opportunities to maximise an award-winning platform; and be part of a dynamic, agile team where you're encouraged to have your own voice and take an active role in growing the business with a bunch of brilliant developers who push the parameters of what's possible?

 

We're looking for a developer with a minimum of 6 months'  C# and ASP.Net to join our shoal to manage and extend the use of relevant 3rd party APIs.

 

In this new role you'll oversee all the API access in and out of Firefish, while identifying opportunities from other software vendors that can be added into the Firefish customer feature-mix.  

 

You're going to love this job if: 

  • You like working in an agile team where you’re responsible for setting your own workload
  • You're looking for a role where you can identify, communicate and justify solution designs to effectively solve development problems through 3rd party API integration
  • You love keeping up-to-date with vendor API updates, factoring in any changes or impact into our development cycle while looking for any opportunity to maximise on new features which would inspire our users to be more successful in their clients' recruitment strategies
  • You enjoy researching solutions, weighing up the positives and negatives of each option provided by 3rd party integration partners

 

We're looking for someone who is persistent, determined and thorough in consistently delivering the highest quality of work.  You enjoy exploring new methods or ways of achieving things through technology, and love keeping yourself updated in the latest trends.  You're excited about what we can achieve through partner API integrations! 

 

With a degree in Computer Science (or a related subject) you have a minimum of 6 months' development experience using C#.Net / ASP.Net platform using the .NET Framework 4.x.  You have knowledge of XML/XSLT/XPATH and a good understanding of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, AJAX/JSON, etc.

 

We’re well established, having grown 60% organically year-on-year, and are fully funded and ready to rock. Your workload is in your own hands - not dictated to you - and you’re not seen as just another ‘cog in the machine’.

 

So, if you want to be part of something ground-breaking, something different…join us!
